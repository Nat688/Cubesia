Shulkers

Shulker Boxes can now be opened directly from your inventory, without needing to place them on the ground first.
Simply carry a Shulker Box in your inventory to access its contents instantly, saving you the time of placing and breaking it every time you need an item.
This feature makes inventory management much faster, more convenient, and more efficient, especially during mining, exploration, or building sessions.


AFKZone

The AFK Zone is a dedicated area designed to reward players even while they are away from their keyboard.
For every minute spent in the AFK Zone, you will receive a random reward. The longer you remain there, the more rewards you will collect, giving you additional chances to obtain valuable resources over time.
You can access the area at any time using the /afk command, which teleports you directly to the AFK Zone.
You are also free to leave the zone whenever you wish to continue exploring, building, trading, fighting, or progressing on the server.
The available rewards and their drop chances are as follows:
5% – 1 Shard
15% – 2 Shards
25% – 3 Shards
40% – 5 Shards
10% – 10 Shards
4% – 25 Shards
1% – 100 Shards
The AFK Zone is a simple way to earn extra rewards while taking a break, making every minute spent online a little more rewarding.


Graves

A grave system is available to protect your items and experience for 10 minutes after death.
Even if you die in lava, your belongings will remain safely stored inside your grave, giving you enough time to return and recover them.
Simply travel back to the location of your death to retrieve everything. Keep in mind that you'll have to make the journey on foot (unless you're using a Handic Card 😄).
When you die, a grave is automatically created at the exact location of your death, and its coordinates are immediately displayed so you can easily find your way back.
Once you reach your grave, you can right-click it to retrieve your items one by one, or use Shift + Right-Click to collect everything instantly.
You can also use the /graves list command to manage your graves. It displays all of your active graves, their locations, and the remaining time before each one disappears, helping you recover your belongings before they are lost forever.
This system provides an extra layer of protection for your valuables, making death much less punishing while still encouraging you to return and recover your equipment.


SellWands

Sell Wands allow you to sell the contents of an entire chest instantly with just a single click, making resource management much faster and more convenient.
Each Sell Wand has its own multiplier, which depends on its rarity and directly increases the amount of money you receive from each sale.
They also have a limited durability that decreases with every use, with higher rarity Sell Wands generally offering both better multipliers and more durability.
To use a Sell Wand, simply left-click a chest to inspect its contents and view the total number of items along with the amount of money you would earn from selling them.
If you are satisfied with the result, simply right-click the chest to sell all eligible items instantly and receive your money immediately.
This system allows you to manage large quantities of items efficiently while maximizing your profits, making Sell Wands an essential tool for farms and automated resource production.


Vaults

Personal Vaults (PVs) are secure private double chests that can only be accessed by their owner, providing a safe place to store your most valuable items.
You can store virtually any type of item inside, including valuable resources, rare equipment, and long-term supplies, while stackable items behave just like they do in normal chests to maximize storage efficiency.
Use the /pv command to open your Personal Vault menu at any time and access your stored items from anywhere on the server.
Inside the /pv menu, you can Shift + Click on a vault to customize its icon, replacing the default barrel with an item of your choice for a cleaner and more personalized interface.
By default unlock 27 Personal Vaults, with VIP rank it's 45 Personal Vaults.


Minions

Minions are automated workers that can perform a wide variety of tasks for you, making resource gathering and production much easier.
They can be connected to a storage system, including regular chests, trapped chests, and barrels, allowing them to automatically store the resources they collect and keeping your farms organized.
Minions can also be rotated to face North, East, South, or West, giving you complete freedom when designing your farms and fitting them into your base.
Every minion can be upgraded through the Minion Level system. They ask for money; they gain experience by carrying out the tasks entrusted to them.
Each minion also requires a dedicated favorite tool to operate correctly. This tool never loses durability and remains permanently usable by the minion.
Minions also require fuel to operate. Once the fuel runs out, the minion will stop working until more fuel is supplied.
Available fuels are: Coal or Charcoal (80 seconds), Blaze Rod (120 seconds), Coal Block (900 seconds), and Lava Bucket (1200 seconds).
To obtain a minion, simply use a Common Key on the Common Crate.


Fish

The server features a complete custom fishing system with dozens of unique fish represented by custom player heads, each with its own rarity, value, and collection progress.
Every fish you catch is automatically recorded in your personal collection, encouraging exploration and giving you new species to discover across the world.
The main command is /fish, which opens the fishing menu and provides access to every available feature, including bait management, your collection, statistics, and more.
Use /fish journal to browse your Fishing Journal, where you can view every available fish, track your discoveries, and monitor your overall completion progress.
Fish can be sold instantly with /sellfish, allowing you to quickly convert your catches into money without needing to visit a shop.
If you have purchased or obtained fishing baits, use /fish applybaits to apply them to your fishing rod and benefit from their special effects while fishing.
During fishing competitions and server events, /fish top displays the live leaderboard, allowing you to see the current rankings and track who is leading the event in real time.
Whether you enjoy collecting every species, earning money through fishing, or competing against other players during events, the custom fishing system offers plenty of progression and rewards for every type of fisherman.


Elevator

Elevators provide a fast and convenient way to travel vertically through your base without using ladders or stairs.
To create an elevator, simply throw an Ender Pearl onto a wool block. Then, place another elevator block above and/or below it to create a connected elevator system.
Once both elevator blocks are in place, simply jump while standing on the lower elevator to travel upward, or sneak on the upper elevator to travel downward instantly.
Both elevator blocks must be the same wool color in order to connect and function correctly.
This system allows you to move quickly between different floors while keeping your builds clean, compact, and easy to navigate.


Cauldron Concrete

Cauldron Concrete makes crafting concrete much faster by allowing you to convert Concrete Powder into regular Concrete without placing each block individually.
Simply hold the desired Concrete Powder, then Shift + Right-Click on a water-filled cauldron to instantly harden it into normal Concrete.
This feature saves a considerable amount of time when building large structures, as you no longer need to place and break every Concrete Powder block manually.
It is a simple but extremely useful quality-of-life improvement for builders and anyone working on large construction projects.


Crying Portal

Nether Portals can also be built using Crying Obsidian, giving you additional flexibility when designing your builds.
A portal made entirely of Crying Obsidian functions exactly like a regular Obsidian portal and can be lit in the same way.
This feature allows you to create unique portal designs while taking advantage of the distinctive appearance and animated texture of Crying Obsidian.
Whether for aesthetics or creativity, Crying Obsidian is a fully valid alternative for constructing Nether Portals on the server.


Durability Ping

Durability Ping helps you avoid accidentally breaking your valuable equipment by warning you when it is close to breaking.
Whenever one of your equipped items falls below 10% of its maximum durability, you will automatically receive a notification reminding you to repair or replace it.
To prevent unnecessary spam, these warnings are protected by a cooldown, ensuring you are only notified occasionally instead of every time the item loses durability.
This quality-of-life feature gives you plenty of time to react, helping you protect your favorite gear and avoid costly accidents during mining, combat, or exploration.


Axolotl Feed

Axolotl Feed lets you feed axolotls directly using Tropical Fish, without needing to capture them in a water bucket first.
Simply use a Tropical Fish on an axolotl to feed or breed it, making the process much faster and more convenient.
The traditional method using a Bucket of Tropical Fish still works exactly as in vanilla Minecraft, so you are free to use whichever method you prefer.
This quality-of-life feature makes breeding and caring for axolotls much easier while preserving the original game mechanics.


Messages Villager / Trader

Villager & Wandering Trader Notifications keep you informed about important events involving villagers and wandering traders.
Whenever a Villager dies, a server message is automatically sent, including the exact coordinates of where the death occurred, making it easier to investigate or react quickly.
Whenever a Wandering Trader spawns, a notification is also broadcast to all players, letting everyone know that a trader has appeared somewhere in the world.
These notifications improve awareness across the server, helping players protect their villagers and ensuring nobody misses the arrival of a Wandering Trader.


Collectible Blocks

Collectible Blocks improve block collection by allowing certain normally fragile or unobtainable blocks to be recovered more easily.
Glass, all Stained Glass variants, every type of Glass Pane, and Ender Chests will always drop themselves when broken, regardless of the tool you use or whether it has the Silk Touch enchantment.
In addition, Budding Amethyst can now be collected with a tool enchanted with Silk Touch, allowing players to move and use this valuable block in their own farms and builds.
These changes make building, decorating, and resource management much more convenient while preserving the usefulness of the Silk Touch enchantment for special blocks.


Jobs

Jobs allow you to earn both money and experience by performing everyday activities such as mining, farming, fishing, building, hunting, and much more.
Use the /jobs command to open the Jobs menu, where you can browse every available profession and manage your progress.
Right-click on a job to open its detailed information page, showing how much experience and money you earn for each supported action, helping you choose the most profitable activities.
Left-click on a job to view its leaderboard, allowing you to compare your progress with other players and see who has reached the highest level in each profession.
Whether you focus on a single profession or level up multiple jobs, the system provides a rewarding way to progress while playing normally on the server.


No Trample

No Crop Trample prevents farmland and crops from being destroyed when players or mobs walk, run, or jump across them.
You can safely move through your farms without worrying about accidentally turning farmland back into dirt or damaging fully grown crops.
This protection is especially useful for large farms, public areas, and multiplayer environments where many players or animals regularly pass through cultivated land.
It also makes harvesting and replanting much less frustrating, allowing you to move freely while working on your fields without constantly repairing damaged farmland.
This protection is particularly valuable on multiplayer servers, where players, animals, and even unexpected situations can easily damage farmland. Whether you're harvesting, sprinting through your base, or welcoming visitors to your farms, your crops will remain completely safe from accidental trampling.


Bank

The Bank provides a secure way to store your money separately from your wallet, helping you better manage your finances.
Open the bank interface at any time using the /bank command.
Inside the menu, click the Emerald on the right to deposit money into your bank account, or the Emerald on the left to withdraw funds whenever you need them.
You can also click the Diamond to upgrade your bank, increasing its maximum storage capacity and unlocking higher interest rates.
Each bank level provides periodic interest on the money stored in your account, up to a maximum balance determined by your current bank level.
Keep in mind that bank taxes also apply to certain transactions, so managing your deposits, withdrawals, and upgrades wisely will help you maximize your long-term profits.


Bounty

The Bounty system allows players to place a cash reward on another player's head, encouraging PvP and creating exciting hunting opportunities across the server.
If a player with an active bounty is killed, the player who eliminates them automatically receives the bounty reward.
To place a bounty, simply use the command /bounty add <player> <amount>, replacing <player> with the target's username and <amount> with the amount of money you wish to offer.
Whether you want revenge, create friendly competition, or simply make PvP more rewarding, the Bounty system adds an extra layer of excitement to every encounter.


Harvest Master

Harvest Master makes farming significantly faster by allowing you to harvest and replant crops with a single action.
Simply right-click on a fully grown crop to harvest it instantly. If you have the required seeds or crop in your inventory, it will be automatically replanted at the same time, keeping your farm ready for the next growth cycle.
This feature works with virtually all harvestable crops, eliminating the need to break each plant and manually place seeds after every harvest.
It is especially useful for large farms, where it saves countless clicks and greatly improves harvesting efficiency while preserving the layout of your fields.
Whether you're farming for food, trading, or resources, Harvest Master lets you collect crops more quickly and spend more time expanding your farm instead of maintaining it.


Enchanted Book

Enchanted Books allow you to extract the enchantments from an item and convert them into individual enchanted books.
Simply place an enchanted item, such as a pickaxe, into the interface to view every enchantment it contains. You can then choose which enchantments you want to turn into books for later use or trading.
Once the process is completed, the selected enchantments are transferred onto enchanted books, while the original item is consumed and permanently removed.
This feature is especially useful for recovering valuable enchantments from powerful equipment, reorganizing your gear, or selling custom enchanted books to other players without having to rely entirely on the enchantment table.
