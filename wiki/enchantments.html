Wiki - Enchantments


Overview

All enchantments can be obtained in Survival mode through several different methods.
The most common method is to use an enchanting table to improve your weapons, armor, and tools using experience and lapis lazuli.
You can also obtain enchanted books by trading with certain villagers, especially librarians.
Finally, enchantments can also be found in loot chests located throughout various in-game structures, making exploration even more rewarding.


Agility

Agility is an enchantment that increases your step height, allowing you to move much more smoothly and walk over blocks without constantly jumping.
Thanks to this enchantment, movement becomes far more comfortable, letting you overcome small obstacles effortlessly while saving time and improving mobility.
It has 2 levels, with each level further enhancing your ability to navigate complex environments and travel more efficiently.
This enchantment is available exclusively on leggings, making it ideal for explorers and adventurers seeking greater mobility and efficiency.


Air Jump

Air Jump is an enchantment that grants an additional jump while falling, providing better control and exceptional mobility in midair.
It is especially useful for avoiding fatal falls, repositioning during combat, or reaching hard-to-access locations, significantly improving both safety and movement.
This enchantment makes vertical movement smoother and more precise, often making the difference in dangerous environments or high-ground battles.
It is available exclusively on boots, making it essential for daring adventurers and acrobatic fighters.


Anti Knockback

Anti Knockback is an enchantment that reduces the knockback you receive when taking damage, allowing you to remain steady and maintain control during combat.
The higher its level, the more resistant you become to powerful attacks and explosions, enabling you to focus on attacking or dodging without being thrown away.
It is particularly effective in close-range combat or against enemies capable of pushing you back with ease.
This enchantment has 4 levels and can be applied to all armor pieces, making it an essential choice for warriors who want to stand their ground.


Attack Speed

Attack Speed is an enchantment that increases your attack speed, allowing you to strike faster and maximize your damage output in combat.
A higher attack speed puts greater pressure on your opponent while reducing their opportunities to counterattack, making your fights more effective.
This enchantment is especially valuable in close-quarters combat and against agile enemies, where every fraction of a second matters.
It has 3 levels and is available exclusively on swords, significantly improving your offensive capabilities.


Burnless

Burnless is an enchantment that reduces the duration of burn effects suffered by the player, lowering the damage caused by fire and lava.
Each armor piece enchanted with Burnless further decreases the amount of time the player remains on fire, providing significant protection.
When a full set of armor is enchanted with Burnless, burn effects disappear almost instantly, offering near-total immunity to fire damage.
This enchantment can be applied to all armor pieces and is especially useful while exploring volcanic regions or lava-filled environments.


Cobweb

Cobweb is an enchantment that creates a cobweb where your projectile lands, greatly slowing down the target.
This effect can temporarily trap enemies, preventing them from escaping, jumping, or moving efficiently.
It is extremely useful for controlling enemy movement, defending strategic locations, or gaining the upper hand during ranged combat.
This enchantment is available exclusively on crossbows, making it ideal for tactical marksmen and defensive playstyles.


Double Hit

Double Hit is an enchantment that grants a chance to perform a second strike immediately after landing a successful attack, increasing your overall damage output.
This bonus hit can trigger on every attack, making your weapon significantly more dangerous during extended fights.
It is especially effective against durable enemies or in fast-paced PvP encounters where every hit counts.
This enchantment can be applied to weapons, greatly improving your offensive potential.


Downfall

Downfall is an enchantment that unleashes a shockwave whenever you hit the ground after falling, knocking back nearby enemies.
The shockwave can also deal damage, allowing you to turn high jumps into devastating attacks.
It is highly effective in elevated battles or when surrounded by multiple enemies.
This enchantment is available exclusively on boots and is perfect for players who enjoy aerial combat.


Eternal

Eternal is an enchantment that prevents enchanted items from disappearing or being destroyed, even in dangerous situations or after the player's death.
Items enchanted with Eternal remain protected under all circumstances, providing peace of mind for your rarest equipment.
This enchantment is particularly valuable for precious or difficult-to-obtain items, ensuring their long-term preservation.
It can be applied to any enchantable item, making your equipment far more reliable.


Fangs

Fangs is an enchantment that summons Evoker Fangs whenever you take damage, automatically striking nearby enemies.
This effect instantly retaliates against attackers, dealing additional damage without requiring any action from the player.
It is especially effective against groups of enemies or when disrupting incoming attacks, making it a valuable defensive enchantment during intense battles.
As soon as an opponent lands a hit, the summoned fangs emerge from the ground to punish nearby targets, helping you maintain control of the fight.
It has 3 levels, with each level increasing the frequency, effectiveness, and overall power of the summoned fangs.
Fangs is available exclusively on leggings, making it a unique enchantment for players looking to strengthen their defensive capabilities while dealing extra damage.


Flight

Flight is an enchantment that allows you to fly with an Elytra without using Firework Rockets, granting complete freedom in the skies.
It makes long-distance travel much easier while providing excellent maneuverability, even in narrow or complex areas.
This enchantment is particularly useful for exploration, bypassing obstacles, or escaping dangerous situations.
It is available exclusively on Elytras.


Freezing

Freezing is an enchantment that inflicts a powerful slowing effect on enemies you hit, reducing their mobility and ability to retaliate.
It is perfect for controlling the battlefield, slowing attackers, and protecting strategic positions against fast opponents.
The higher the level, the stronger the slowing effect becomes, providing a major advantage in combat.
It has 2 levels and is available exclusively on swords.


Gravity

Gravity is an enchantment that applies the Levitation effect to enemies you strike, causing them to float helplessly into the air.
It allows you to control your opponents, making them vulnerable to combos and ranged attacks.
Higher levels increase both the duration and strength of the levitation effect, offering superior battlefield control.
It has 2 levels and is available exclusively on swords.


Health

Health is an enchantment that increases your maximum health, making you more resistant to attacks and dangerous environments.
Each level significantly boosts your endurance, allowing you to survive much longer during difficult battles or hazardous expeditions.
It is ideal for players who want to improve their survivability and confidently take greater risks.
It has 5 levels and can be applied to all armor pieces.


Leaping

Leaping is an enchantment that increases your jump height, allowing you to clear obstacles more easily and reach elevated locations.
It improves your mobility, making it easier to escape enemies or attack from advantageous positions.
This effect is especially useful for exploration and strategic combat that requires quick movement.
This enchantment is available exclusively on boots.


Life Steal

Life Steal is an enchantment that restores a portion of your health whenever you deal damage to an enemy, turning your attacks into a constant source of healing.
Each level increases the amount of health restored, making you far more durable during extended fights, especially against multiple opponents.
This enchantment is particularly effective in close combat, allowing you to stay in battle longer while gradually weakening your enemies.
It has 3 levels and can be applied to weapons, providing an excellent balance between offense and survivability.


Longevity

Longevity is an enchantment that increases the durability of enchanted items, reducing wear and extending their lifespan.
It is especially useful for frequently used tools and armor, helping you save resources by reducing the need for repairs or replacements.
Each level further improves durability, making your equipment more reliable for long mining sessions, exploration, and combat.
This enchantment can be applied to any enchantable item.


Magnetic

Magnetic is an enchantment that automatically attracts mined blocks and nearby dropped items directly to you, making resource collection much more efficient.
It is compatible with Smelting and Veinminer, allowing you to gather resources quickly and effortlessly.
This enchantment reduces the time spent collecting drops while greatly improving your overall harvesting efficiency.
It can be applied to both tools and weapons, making it invaluable for miners and adventurers alike.


Path Speed

Path Speed is an enchantment that increases your movement speed while walking on dirt paths, allowing you to travel more quickly and efficiently.
It is ideal for exploring large areas, escaping enemies, or simply moving smoothly through villages and countryside.
Each level further boosts your movement speed, reducing travel time and improving overall mobility.
It has 3 levels and is available exclusively on boots.


Poisoning

Poisoning is an enchantment that inflicts the Poison effect on enemies you strike, dealing damage over time and gradually weakening them.
Each level increases both the duration and effectiveness of the poison, making opponents more vulnerable in combat.
It is especially useful for crowd control, softening enemies before engaging them directly, and winning prolonged battles.
It has 2 levels and is available exclusively on swords.


Projectile Deflection

Projectile Deflection is an enchantment that reduces the damage taken from projectiles, providing improved protection against ranged attacks.
It is especially useful when fighting skeletons, pillagers, or any enemy relying on ranged weapons.
This enchantment helps you stay alive longer during ranged encounters by significantly reducing incoming damage.
It is available exclusively on chestplates.


Reach

Reach is an enchantment that increases your interaction range with blocks and enemies, allowing you to mine, attack, and interact from farther away.
It is especially useful for mining safely, striking enemies before they can reach you, and interacting with difficult-to-access blocks.
Each additional level increases your reach, giving you greater control in every situation.
It has 3 levels and can be applied to mining tools and weapons.


Smelting

Smelting is an enchantment that automatically smelts mined ores, instantly converting them into ingots or other usable materials.
It greatly reduces processing time and improves efficiency while mining and gathering resources.
Compatible with Veinminer and Magnetic, it allows you to combine fast mining with automatic collection for maximum productivity.
This enchantment is available exclusively on pickaxes.


Spectral

Spectral is an enchantment that gives your projectiles a glowing effect, making them easier to follow and improving the accuracy of your shots.
It is especially useful in dark environments such as caves or during the night, where projectile visibility is limited.
Every shot becomes easier to track, helping you locate enemies and aim more accurately even over long distances.
This enchantment can be applied to bows, crossbows, and tridents, providing a valuable advantage in ranged combat.


Spiky

Spiky is an enchantment that damages enemies whenever they attack your shield, turning your defense into an automatic counterattack.
Each level increases the reflected damage, making your shield both a defensive and offensive tool.
It is particularly effective against aggressive enemies and in PvP, punishing anyone who attacks you directly.
It has 3 levels and is available exclusively on shields.


Swiftness

Swiftness is an enchantment that increases your movement speed, allowing you to travel faster across every type of terrain.
It improves exploration, combat mobility, and strategic retreats, giving you a significant movement advantage.
Each level grants an additional speed bonus, making your character even faster and harder to catch.
It has 4 levels and is available exclusively on boots.


Thunder Strike

Thunder Strike is an enchantment that summons lightning whenever you land a fully charged attack, dealing devastating electrical damage to nearby enemies.
It is especially effective against groups of enemies or powerful opponents, adding both spectacular visuals and tremendous damage to your attacks.
Each level increases the frequency and power of the lightning strikes.
It has 3 levels and is available exclusively on the Mace.


Timber

Timber is an enchantment that allows you to chop down all connected logs of the same tree at once, dramatically speeding up wood collection.
It is especially useful for large building projects or whenever you need to gather wood quickly without breaking every log individually.
This enchantment works perfectly with Magnetic, automatically collecting the harvested logs and reducing unnecessary movement.
It is available exclusively on axes, making it an essential enchantment for efficient forestry.


Toughness

Toughness is an enchantment that increases your armor's toughness and durability, reducing the damage you receive from enemy attacks.
It is particularly useful for surviving prolonged battles or fighting powerful opponents.
Each level grants additional toughness, making your armor even stronger and more reliable.
It has 3 levels and can be applied to every armor piece.


True Shot

True Shot is an enchantment that allows your arrows to ignore gravity, keeping them on a perfectly straight trajectory over long distances.
Every shot becomes much more accurate, making it easier to hit distant enemies without constantly adjusting your aim.
It is especially valuable for long-range combat where precision is essential.
This enchantment can be applied to bows, crossbows, and tridents.


Undying

Undying is an enchantment that protects the player like a Totem of Undying, absorbing damage that would otherwise be fatal.
When triggered, it can save you from certain death, allowing you to continue fighting or escape dangerous situations.
It is especially valuable during difficult encounters or against powerful enemies, providing a precious second chance.
This enchantment is available exclusively on shields.


Veinminer

Veinminer is an enchantment that allows you to mine multiple connected blocks of the same ore vein at once, greatly increasing mining speed and efficiency.
It is compatible with Fortune, Silk Touch, Smelting, and Magnetic, maximizing both resource yield and automatic collection.
Perfect for dedicated miners, it drastically reduces mining time while optimizing resource gathering.
This enchantment is available exclusively on pickaxes.


Vision

Vision is an enchantment that grants the Night Vision effect, dramatically improving visibility in dark environments and underground areas.
With this enchantment, exploring caves, mines, or nighttime landscapes becomes much easier without relying on torches or other light sources.
It is especially useful for spotting hostile mobs, rare ores, or hidden passages, providing a significant advantage during exploration and combat.
This enchantment is available exclusively on helmets, turning your headgear into a permanent and reliable source of Night Vision.


Withering

Withering is an enchantment that inflicts the Wither effect on enemies you hit, dealing damage over time while steadily weakening them.
It is particularly effective against powerful enemies, softening them before direct engagements or during prolonged battles.
Each level increases both the duration and strength of the Wither effect, providing a considerable strategic advantage.
It has 2 levels and is available exclusively on swords.


XP Boost

XP Boost is an enchantment that increases the amount of experience you gain from your activities, including combat, mining, and exploration.
It is extremely useful for leveling up faster and improving your equipment or enchantments more efficiently.
Each additional level further increases the experience bonus, allowing for faster and more rewarding progression.
This enchantment can be applied to both tools and weapons.
