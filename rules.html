Cubesia rules

Minecraft server rules

Article A: Gameplay

  • Exploiting bugs is prohibited. Any bugs must be reported in the bug-report channel on our Discord server.
  • Any form of cheating is strictly prohibited (TP-kill, mods providing an unfair advantage, auto-sell, etc.). Anti-AFK mods are allowed.

Article B: Your Account

  • You are fully responsible for your Minecraft account.
  • No compensation or refund will be provided if you lend your account to someone else.
  • Inappropriate usernames (racist, offensive, political, homophobic, etc.) and inappropriate skins (political, nudity, etc.) are not allowed.

Article C: Chat Rules

  • All discussions must remain respectful.
  • Changing the default text font or typography is not allowed.
  • The server's primary language is French. However, English is tolerated for English-speaking players.
  • The following messages are prohibited:
    • Provocation, insults, or harassment directed at an individual or community.
    • Serious discriminatory or hateful content, including racism, misogyny, homophobia, transphobia, or political propaganda.
    • Suggestive or inappropriate content (sexual, racist, etc.).
    • Topics likely to start arguments (sports, politics, religion, geopolitical conflicts, etc.).
    • Trolling or obscene content.
    • Spam (repeated messages or words), flooding (repeating the same character or emoji excessively, e.g. Thankssssssssssssss), and excessive use of CAPITAL LETTERS.
    • Threats, harassment, dark humor, sharing private information, requesting social media accounts, or similar behavior.
  • These rules apply everywhere on the server, including:
    • Public chat.
    • Private messages.
    • Signs.
    • Renamed items.
    • And any other form of communication.
  • Posting links in chat is prohibited.
  • Advertising anything that competes with or harms Cubesia is prohibited.

Article D: Discord

  • Discord is AllPlugs' primary communication platform. Joining it is highly recommended to receive update announcements, other important information, and to create support tickets.
  • If you encounter a problem, have a question, want to make a suggestion, or need to report a bug, please use Discord. Contacting staff members directly through private messages is not allowed.

Discord serveur rules

Part I: Your Profile

Article A: Profile Pictures

  • Your profile picture must not contain obscene, explicit, or political content.
  • Profile pictures must not be offensive toward any individual or group.
  • Using another user's personal photograph as your profile picture is prohibited.
  • Any image containing sexual or explicit content is prohibited.

Article B: Usernames

  • Your username must not reference historical or political figures.
  • Your username must begin with a standard keyboard character so it can be mentioned properly.
  • Displaying another player's personal or private information in your username is prohibited.

Article C: Statuses & Bios

  • Your status and bio must not contain political content or encourage hatred.
  • Links leading to scams or malicious websites are prohibited in your bio.
  • Any form of disrespect toward another person may result in sanctions.

Part II: Text Channels

Article A: Spam & Chat Pollution

  • Just like in-game, spam and flooding are prohibited.
  • Posting links other than the AllPlugs website, the server IP, or the server's official social media pages is prohibited.
  • Excessively mentioning roles or members is strictly prohibited.
  • Pinging staff members is strictly prohibited, whether in tickets or public chat.

Article B: Expected Conduct

  • You are expected to remain respectful when interacting with other players.
  • In support tickets, insults, provocation, mockery, or false accusations may result in sanctions, including permanent bans from both the Minecraft server and Discord.
  • Good manners are expected, especially when speaking with staff members.
  • Saying "Hello" when opening a support ticket is always appreciated 😉.

Article C: Discussion Topics

  • As with your profile, political discussions are not allowed.
  • You must not insult or disrespect other players.
  • Racism, homophobia, xenophobia, and all other forms of hate speech are strictly prohibited.
  • Any form of harassment is prohibited and will result in severe sanctions.

Part III: Voice Channels

Article A: Expected Conduct

  • Just like in text chat, you must remain respectful toward other players.
  • You are free to discuss any appropriate topic, but please be mindful of the age of the people in your voice channel.
  • If you wish to have a private conversation, you may use one of the limited-access voice channels or create a private voice channel.

Article B: Prohibited Behavior

  • Shouting into your microphone with the intention of annoying other users is prohibited.
  • Using a soundboard to disturb or annoy other users is prohibited.
  • Sharing personal or private information over voice chat is prohibited.
