Wiki - Commands

  • /afk Teleports you to the AFK zone.
  • /bin or /disposal or /trash Allows you to destroy your items without causing lag.
  • /home Open the GUI to view, set, and delete your homes.
  • /spawn Teleports you to the spawn.
  • /settings Opens a GUI allowing you to toggle messages/features.
  • /jobs or /skills or /skill Allows you to view jobs using a GUI.
  • /pv or /vault Opens your personal player vault.
  • /msg or /tell Allows you to send private messages.
  • /profil or /profile Opens your profile.
  • /grave list Gives you the list of your graves.
  • /minions stats/statistics Lets you view your minions stats.
  • /tpa Allows you to teleport to players.
  • /tpahere Allows you to teleport a player to you.
  • /tpa accept/cancel or /tpahere accept/cancel Allows you to accept or cancel a teleportation request.

  • /craft Opens a cratingtable.
  • /ec or /enderchest Opens an enderchest.
  • /anvil Opens an anvil.
  • /grindstone Opens a grindstone.
  • /smithingtable or /smithing Open a smithingtable.
  • /stonecutter Open a stonecutter.
  • /loom Open a loom.

  • /discord Provide the link to the Discord.
  • /wiki Provide the link to the Wiki.
  • /rules or /rule Provide the link to the Rules.
  • /site or /website Provide the link to the WebSite.

  • /money Provides your current balance in money.
  • /money top List the top players in terms of money.
  • /shards Provides your current balance in shards.
  • /shards top List the top players in terms of shards.
  • /pay Paid the other players.
  • /paytoggle Toggle payments from other players.
  • /bank Opens the bank GUI.
  • /banktop Gives you the bank's current top ranking.
  • /bounty or /boutnies Open the mistletoe using the players' bounties.
  • /bounty add or /bounties add Add a player bounty.

  • /shop Open the server shop.
  • /sell or /sellgui Allows you to sell your items (except custom fish).
  • /sellprice Opens a GUI to view item prices (tiers in alphabetical order)
  • /sellall Allows you to sell your entire inventory.
  • /autosell Enables or disables autosell (automatically sells selected items).

  • /fish Opens the custom fish interface.
  • /sellfish Allows you to sell your fish.
  • /fish journal Opens the fish collection interface.
  • /fish applybaits Allows you to apply purchased or crafted bait to your fishing rod.
  • /fish sellall Allows you to sell all your fish at once.
  • /fish top Allows you to view the top rankings (only during events).
  • /fish toggle Enables or disables custom fishing.

VIP ONLY

  • /fly Allows you to fly just like in Creative mode.
  • /heal Fully regenerate your health.
  • /feed Fully replenish your hunger
